# v0.1.3 Post-session Questionnaire

Participation and every question are voluntary. Record a bounded rating or de-identified summary only. Do not record names, contact details, exact ages, addresses, usernames, quotations that identify a participant, private roles/objectives, room codes, IP/network details, device identifiers, report contents, health information, audio, photographs, or video.

1. From 1–5, how clear was extracting and launching the verified candidate?
2. From 1–5, how clear was controller-first joining and roster confirmation?
3. In a short de-identified summary, what did the public objective seem to be?
4. Where, if anywhere, was expected input unclear?
5. Were prompts, votes, and private-reveal directions understandable without exposing private information?
6. Could Help, reconnect/reset guidance, Build & Support, and report export be found?
7. Was text readable from the actual seating/viewing arrangement? This is not accessibility certification.
8. Did afterlife participation remain understandable? Do not record private role details.
9. Were ending, local export, and rematch/return-to-title clear?
10. What one bounded operability or presentation issue should be considered separately?

Do not ask whether the session proves the game is fun, balanced, accessible, secure, private, or correctly timed. One pilot cannot establish those conclusions.

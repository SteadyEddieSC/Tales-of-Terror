## Relationship to production Tale schema

P0.9 does not change production package kind `tale` or schema version `1`.

It does not modify:

- `tools/tale_package.py`;
- `tools/tale_catalog.py`;
- `game/src/session/tale_package.gd`;
- `game/src/session/tale_catalog.gd`;
- the static provider registry;
- the production Tale catalog;
- `game/data/tales/lantern_house`.

The authoring package lives only under `docs/` and is not a runtime-loader input.

## Validation coverage

The validator rejects:

- unknown or missing fields;
- runtime, compilation, or catalog authorization;
- authoring files placed in the production Tale directory;
- unsafe, absolute, generated, executable, network, or missing references;
- unresolved Design Bible anchors;
- unknown P0.8 traceability IDs;
- missing governed media files;
- duplicate or unstable IDs;
- mode and stage registry drift;
- missing cooperative fallback;
- unreachable stages or terminal state;
- nondeterministic or repeatable High Water transformation;
- incomplete stage, tide, ending, or 120-ID inventory;
- hazards without warning/response/recovery;
- encounters without legal choices;
- roles without generic alternatives;
- forms without stable-seat continuation;
- endings without explicit outcome attribution;
- fewer than three explicit human-review obligations;
- removal of all production-blocking open decisions;
- any change to the no-compilation boundary.

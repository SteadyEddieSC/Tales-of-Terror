## Authority and boundaries

These documents preserve preproduction work ahead of later implementation. They do not authorize a second production Tale, gameplay code, catalog or package changes, snapshot or report schema changes, companion protocol changes, balance claims, human-playtest claims, final artwork, final audio, or final branding.

Lantern House remains the sole production Tale. Terror Turn and The Underteller remain provisional names pending issue #7.

Until the project owner explicitly states otherwise, Codex/local implementation and human playtesting are considered blocked. Preproduction may continue through documentation, dialogue, visual development, audio direction, asset creation, storyboards, schemas, and implementation-ready backlog preparation.

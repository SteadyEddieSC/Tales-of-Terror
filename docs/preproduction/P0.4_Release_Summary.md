# P0.4 — Drowned Harbor Audio Direction & SFX Brief Pack

**Status:** Release candidate on PR #68
**Baseline:** `e0cc8932904569f2b4b9b918da7d2727984464de`
**Scope:** Preproduction audio direction, ambience/SFX briefs, privacy and accessibility requirements, and validation tooling only

## Delivered

- Drowned Harbor audio-direction foundation.
- Sonic north star: a town remembering itself through water, timber, bells, rope, glass, and damaged records.
- Mechanical-truth and no-audio-only-information rules.
- Speech, decision, and critical-cue hierarchy.
- Original-first signature sound policy.
- Shared-speaker public/private information boundaries.
- Spooky, Grim, and Gore & Dread audio-profile guidance.
- Stage ambience direction for Low Tide, Bellhouse Ledger, Lighthouse Council, High Water, and Last Light.
- Provisional spatial classes, bus hierarchy, ducking principles, loop rules, mono-safety, captions, visual redundancy, reduced dynamics, and source-master targets.
- Machine-readable audio-asset brief schema.
- 36 governed audio briefs across three manifests:
  - 8 ambience beds;
  - 12 signature and gameplay event cues;
  - 16 bargain, Restless-continuation, hazard, seat-control, admission, and ending cues.
- Generation-ready prompts, negative constraints, duration budgets, mix policy, variation requirements, provenance policy, and non-approval boundaries.
- Standard-library cross-manifest validator.
- Regression tests covering structural, originality, privacy, accessibility, duration, spatial, prompt, and licensing failures.
- Dedicated GitHub Actions validation.
- Repository guide defining scope, generation, purchasing, privacy, and deferred work.

## Boundaries

- No generated or recorded production audio.
- No final music, adaptive score, Underteller voice, voice casting, or recorded dialogue.
- No final loudness, bus, middleware, Godot import, spatialization, television-speaker, headphone, surround, fatigue, or accessibility claim.
- No marketplace, Humble, library, subscription, commissioned, or AI-assisted sound is approved for production.
- Private gameplay state must not be encoded into shared-speaker audio.
- Drowned Harbor remains design-only.
- Lantern House remains the sole production Tale.
- Codex/local Windows implementation and human listening/playtesting remain blocked until explicitly reopened by the project owner.
- Terror Turn and The Underteller remain provisional pending issue #7.

## Validation expectations

The exact candidate must pass:

- Repository checks;
- Preproduction audio asset validation;
- Godot 4.7 tests;
- Portable internal playtest builds.

The Companion dependency-audit failure remains separately tracked by issue #44 and is not changed or suppressed by this package.

## Audition boundary

P0.4 intentionally stops before broad audio generation. A later governed candidate batch should select a small number of signature cues, identify the exact generator or recording method, preserve tool terms and provenance, and produce external audition files without calling them production audio.

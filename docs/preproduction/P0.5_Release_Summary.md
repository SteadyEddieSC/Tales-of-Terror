## Boundaries

- No final composition or recording.
- No production stems, loops, transitions, mix, loudness, mastering, middleware, or Godot integration.
- No final Underteller interaction tuning.
- No generated, commissioned, licensed, marketplace, subscription, or AI-assisted candidate is approved as production music.
- Content ID registration is prohibited in P0.5.
- Music-off mode must preserve full gameplay understanding.
- Music may not replace P0.4 critical sound effects or encode required information.
- Drowned Harbor remains design-only.
- Lantern House remains the sole production Tale.
- Codex/local Windows implementation and human listening/playtesting remain blocked until explicitly reopened by the project owner.
- Terror Turn and The Underteller remain provisional pending issue #7.

## Validation expectations

The exact candidate must pass:

- Repository checks;
- Preproduction music asset validation;
- Godot 4.7 tests;
- Portable internal playtest builds.

The Companion dependency-audit failure remains separately tracked by issue #44 and is not changed or suppressed by this package.

## Candidate-composition boundary

P0.5 intentionally stops before broad music generation or subscription purchase.

A later candidate package should:

- select a small signature subset;
- identify the exact human, tool, model, or commissioned method;
- verify current commercial-game and post-cancellation rights;
- record Content ID and claims behavior;
- preserve prompts, source records, and human edits;
- produce external audition files and stems;
- compare dialogue masking, loop fatigue, originality, adaptive editability, mono behavior, and reduced-density performance;
- avoid treating generated files as production music.

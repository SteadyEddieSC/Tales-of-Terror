# ADR-0008-working-title — Use Terror Turn as the preferred working title

- **Status:** Accepted as a working title; clearance pending
- **Date:** July 11, 2026

## Context

The previous working title was too tied to a literal book premise and was not the preferred direction. The game needs a short identity that supports board-game turns, reversals, transformations, and multiple horror settings.

## Decision

Use **Terror Turn** in game-facing documents, prototypes, and internal planning. Keep the repository name `Tales-of-Terror` temporarily. Do not buy a domain, rename the repository, or submit to a storefront until a careful trademark, storefront, domain, social-handle, and common-law review is complete.

## Alternatives

Terrorbound, Turn of Terror, Horror Unwritten, The Terror Ledger, and other candidates remain fallback options.

## Consequences

All canonical text and milestone exports must use Terror Turn while clearly marking it as a working title. An informal search is not legal clearance.

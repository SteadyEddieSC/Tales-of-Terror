## Candidate B — Gemini

### Strengths

- Strong prompt adherence: one image, no text, and near-16:9 output.
- Excellent landmark separation and thumbnail readability.
- Clearly inked, illustrated, and non-photorealistic.
- Travelers, causeway, Bellhouse, town, and lighthouse are easy to identify.
- Useful reference for the Spooky presentation profile and for board-state clarity.

### Weaknesses

- Far too bright and desaturated for the primary key-art mood.
- Flat values reduce tactile depth and miniature-diorama presence.
- Wet timber, oxidized brass, salt glass, and black tidal mud do not feel material-specific enough.
- The harbor appears abandoned and melancholy more than supernatural or dangerous.
- The lighthouse is close to centered and the Bellhouse/lifeboat geography remains simplified.
- The Salt Market is not distinctive.

### Disposition

Retain as a style and readability reference, especially for Spooky-profile concepts. Do not use as the primary key-art north star.

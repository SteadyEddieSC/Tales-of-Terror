# v0.0.6 Turn, Event & Card Engine

## Delivered

- Scene-independent `RulesSession` authority for 1–8 stable seats, configurable phases, prompts, events, deterministic RNG, votes, cards, inventory, terminal state, and ordered history.
- Validated declarative event/card schema with bounded conditions/effects and an automated event-ID-branch guard.
- Atomic consequence preflight with all Living Board effects routed through `BoardState`.
- Seeded checks and deck shuffles with auditable RNG counters.
- Versioned JSON-compatible rules snapshots with atomic content/reference validation.
- Replaceable `scenario_host` presentation payloads and a controller-first, symbol-supported safe-frame HUD/diagnostic view.
- A compact Lantern House rules sequence demonstrating choices, a public vote, a check, card play, inventory, follow-up events, two board mutations, and a terminal result.

## Validation

The pinned Godot 4.7 workflow runs the new Turn/Event/Card suite after all v0.0.2–v0.0.5 regressions. Local evidence and manual Compatibility-renderer results are recorded in the draft PR.

## Intentionally deferred

Dread Director selection/pacing, roles/factions/afterlife, combat, procedural generation, networking/companions/private choices, campaign saves, and final host branding/assets remain out of scope.

# v0.0.7 Dread Director

## Delivered

- Local, scene-independent deterministic Director with validated authored profiles and intervention candidates; no cloud or LLM dependency.
- Read-only normalized telemetry copied from `RulesSession` and `BoardState`, with no personal/device data or wall-clock surveillance.
- Separately salted Director RNG, explainable integer score components, ineligible reasons, stable targeting, and replayable decisions.
- Explicit budgets, spacing, cooldowns, repetition penalties, pressure caps, disconnect exclusion, per-seat targeting caps, mercy, recovery windows, and authored no-op behavior.
- Proposal-only runtime plus an authority adapter for atomic RulesSession, BoardState, host/ambient, and no-op applications.
- Versioned Director snapshots, bounded audit history, five authored modes, and separate friendly player HUD/paged raw diagnostics.
- Lantern House struggling, cruising, stalled, no-op, rules/board, and presentation demonstrations.
- Comprehensive unit coverage and a practical 90-sequence multi-seed/profile/trajectory simulation in pinned Godot 4.7 CI.

## Validation intent

The exit gate is reproducible and visibly distinct pacing for struggling, cruising, and stalled groups while deck/check RNG remains unchanged by Director evaluation. Windows Compatibility-renderer captures and local/GitHub check results are recorded on the protected draft PR.

## Intentionally deferred

Roles/factions/afterlife, full combat, networking/companions, campaign saves, procedural generation, cloud AI/LLMs, final host branding/assets, production balance, and long-term player profiling remain out of scope.

# v0.1.0 First Vertical Slice

## Delivered

- One versioned, validated Lantern House manifest and typed orchestration coordinator over the existing native authorities.
- A controller-first title-to-ending player flow for 1–8 stable seats, with keyboard fallback, authored cooperative fallback at one or two seats, complete no-phone play, optional filtered companions, and clean rematch/return.
- Five bounded tale stages covering exploration features, Living Board mutation/reveal/occupancy, event/prompt/vote/check/card/inventory flow, one Director opportunity, defeated-to-Restless continuation, deterministic mixed outcomes, and privacy-safe ending presentation.
- Atomic initialization/stage/restore behavior, versioned snapshots, canonical deterministic replay digests, focused GUT and standalone coverage, and a 24-run 1–8-seat simulation across three seeds.

## Authority and product position

Godot remains authoritative. The coordinator holds references and routes requests; it does not duplicate mutable board, rules, Director, role, exploration, seat, or companion state. Browsers remain optional filtered surfaces. The release preserves Godot 4.7.1-stable, Compatibility, 960×540, dependency locks, vendored GUT, provisional branding, and zero-finding style gates.

## Deferred and unclaimed

Physical controllers, phones, household Wi-Fi, television-distance review, native physical 4K, router/firewall variants, live Cloudflare, penetration/security certification, balance/fun certification, and long-session observation remain deferred.
